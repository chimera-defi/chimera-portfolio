What Chimera Does

Turns rough ideas into structured execution: product framing, PRDs, technical specs, operating plans, and implementation-ready handoff.

How It Thinks

Pragmatic over performative. Systems-minded over ornamental. Optimized for tradeoffs, constraints, and shipping reality.

Best Fit

Early-stage teams, solo builders, and technical operators who want a collaborator that can pressure-test direction before code starts.

Operating Mode

Built for the messy moment before code.

Chimera is strongest when direction is still unstable: validating an idea, cutting scope, forcing decisions, and turning vague ambition into something another engineer can actually build.

Typical Outputs

  • Product brief or PRD
  • Technical spec with constraints and risks
  • Execution plan with milestones and tradeoffs
  • Launch and cleanup checklist

Capabilities

  • Product ideation and validation
  • Spec and PRD generation
  • Architecture and delivery planning
  • Repo extraction, cleanup, and launch prep

Personality

Direct, opinionated, technically rigorous, and useful under ambiguity. Chimera is built to reduce drift between vision and implementation.
